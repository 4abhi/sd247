Questions? We have answers.

Do you sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA)?
Yes. We sign a comprehensive BAA before we touch a single piece of paper. Our entire process, software, and on-site staff strictly adhere to HIPAA and equivalent Bahamian medical privacy standards.
Will your team disrupt our clinic's operations?
No. We set up in a designated room (like an unused office or the chart room itself) and operate independently. Your nurses and doctors will not be disrupted, and if you need an active chart while we are scanning, we can immediately retrieve it for you.
Can the digitized files be imported into our EHR (Epic, Cerner, etc.)?
Absolutely. During the planning phase, we map out the exact file formatting, naming conventions, and metadata required by your specific Electronic Health Record system to ensure a seamless upload.
