Questions? We have answers.

How do you protect attorney-client privilege?
We process everything 100% on-site at your firm. We sign a strict Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before beginning work, and our staff are highly vetted. Because documents never leave your physical control, the chain of custody and privilege are absolutely preserved.
Will your team disrupt our paralegals or associates?
No. We set up our high-speed equipment in a designated room (like an unused office or the file room itself) and operate independently. Your team can work without interruption. If an attorney urgently needs a file while we are scanning, we will immediately retrieve it for them.
Can the digitized files be imported into Clio, MyCase, or Relativity?
Yes. All files are processed with Optical Character Recognition (OCR), making them fully text-searchable. During our planning phase, we structure the naming conventions and metadata to allow for direct, seamless upload into your specific legal practice management or eDiscovery software.
