Frequently Asked Questions

Do you operate outside of Nassau?
Yes. While we are headquartered in Nassau, our specialized mobile digitization teams can deploy to Freeport and across the Family Islands to digitize your records directly on-site at your facility.
How is pricing structured?
Our pricing is highly transparent and based entirely on the estimated volume of documents (usually calculated by banker's boxes or filing cabinet drawers), the level of OCR required, and the specific metadata/integration needs of your software systems. We provide a firm, fixed quote before beginning any work.
Are you hiring?
We are always looking for highly vetted, detail-oriented professionals to join our on-site deployment teams. If you have experience in document management, compliance, or IT administration, please email your resume to careers@securedocs247.com.
